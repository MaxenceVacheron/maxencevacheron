Legend Of Zelda - The Hyrule Castle : A Bash Story >_

The game is a turn-by-turn RPG.
You incarnate a character that challenges the Hyrule Castle, a tower composed of 10 floors. Each floor you encounter an enemy and on the last floor, you challenge the Boss.
During each fight, you have the choice between "Attack" and "Heal".
The Character is randomly chosen and the enemies and Boss are picked accordingly.
This project allowed me to have a better understanding of BASH.

CLI demo only works on Desktop.
